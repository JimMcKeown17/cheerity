About Us

Giving Children Hope

Cheerity brings hope to impoverished children in Joe Slovo, South Africa, by providing essential school shoes and uniforms. These gifts not only offer dignity but also empower children to attend school with pride and confidence. Join us in making a meaningful difference in their lives.

Joe Slovo

Joe Slovo is an informal settlement in Langa, and in Milnerton Cape Town. Like many other informal settlements, it was named after former anti-Apartheid activist, Joe Slovo. With over 2000,000 residents, Joe Slovo is one of the largest and most impoverished informal settlements in South Africa. Tens of thousands of families are living in wooden and tin shacks, struggling to survive every day.

Our Founder

Likhanye Ndima

Likhanye Ndima, the dedicated founder of Cheerity, has been passionately running the organization for the past two years. Fueled by her unwavering love and belief in children, Likhanye has selflessly invested her own money to purchase school shoes and uniforms for dozens of children in need. She firmly believes that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty and is committed to ensuring that every child can attend school with dignity and grace. Through Cheerity, Likhanye strives to provide the foundational support that children need to succeed and build brighter futures.
